## Team Vision
Team **Hashira** aims to build a high-quality, innovative software project through collaboration, professionalism, and continuous learning.  
Our vision is to strengthen our technical and teamwork skills by applying best practices in software development and agile collaboration.

---

## Team Goals
1. Develop a reliable software product using **Kotlin** and standard engineering practices.  
2. Promote teamwork, accountability, and shared ownership.  
3. Ensure timely delivery with high-quality and maintainable code.  
4. Foster continuous learning and improvement through regular reviews.

---

## Communication Rules
- Official communication channels: **Slack** or **WhatsApp**.  
- **Weekly meeting** every **Monday at 2:00 PM** to discuss progress and blockers.  
- Each member posts a **weekly update** about their completed or ongoing tasks.  
- Communication should always be **respectful, clear, and constructive**.

---

## Coding Standards
- Follow **Kotlin coding conventions**.  
- Write **clear and concise comments** explaining complex functions and logic.  
- Use meaningful variable and function names.  
- Every code change must be **reviewed by another team member** before merging.  
- Ensure code quality, performance, and security in all contributions.

---

## Git Branching Strategy
- Main branch: **main**  
- For new features: `feature-[feature-name]`  
- For bug fixes: `bugfix-[bug-description]`  
- All changes must go through a **Pull Request (PR)** before merging.  
- PRs must be reviewed and approved by at least one team member.

---

---

## Decision-Making Process
- Technical and project-related decisions are made through **team voting**.  
- In case of a tie, the **Repository Manager** or **Team Lead** has the final say.  
- All major decisions must be documented in the team’s public communication channel for transparency.

---

## Team Values
- **Commitment** – Deliver tasks on time and with quality.  
- **Respect** – Communicate politely and value each opinion.  
- **Transparency** – Share information openly and honestly.  
- **Collaboration** – Work as one cohesive unit.  
- **Growth** – Learn from challenges and feedback.

---

## Charter Review
This team charter will be reviewed and updated **monthly** or whenever the project scope or structure changes.
